About NovaClean

At NovaClean, we combine expert knowledge, cutting-edge technology, and eco-friendly methods to deliver the highest standard of indoor air quality services in Halifax. With over 10 years of experience, our certified technicians ensure your home’s air ducts and HVAC systems are spotless and safe.

We pride ourselves on transparency, punctuality, and customer satisfaction. Your health and comfort are our top priority.

Air Quality Tips

  • Change filters regularly: Every 1–3 months depending on use.
  • Inspect vents for mold: Especially in basements and bathrooms.
  • Keep vents clear: Avoid blocking airflow with furniture.
