About NovaClean

At NovaClean, we combine expert knowledge, cutting-edge technology, and eco-friendly methods to deliver the highest standard of indoor air quality services in Halifax. With over 10 years of experience, our certified technicians ensure your home’s air ducts and HVAC systems are spotless and safe.

We pride ourselves on transparency, punctuality, and customer satisfaction. Your health and comfort are our top priority.

Our Step-By-Step Process

  1. Step 1: Schedule a Consultation

    Book an appointment online or call us to set a convenient time.

  2. Step 2: Comprehensive Inspection

    Our experts conduct a detailed assessment of your ducts and HVAC system.

  3. Step 3: Deep Cleaning & Treatment

    Using specialized equipment, we remove all dirt, mold, and allergens.

  4. Step 4: Post-Cleaning Air Quality Test

    We verify the improvement with advanced air quality testing devices.

  5. Step 5: Expert Maintenance Tips

    Receive personalized recommendations to keep your air system in top shape year-round.
