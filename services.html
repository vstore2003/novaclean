Why Choose NovaClean?

At NovaClean, we are committed to improving your indoor air quality and HVAC system performance through eco-friendly, thorough, and certified duct cleaning and maintenance services. With over 10 years of trusted experience, our technicians use advanced equipment and proven methods to eliminate dust, allergens, mold, and contaminants, enhancing your comfort and health.

Our Exclusive Services

Premium Duct Cleaning

Our comprehensive duct cleaning service removes built-up dust, allergens, and contaminants that reduce your HVAC efficiency and compromise air quality. Using state-of-the-art vacuum and brushing tools, we clean every duct thoroughly, promoting healthier air circulation and reducing allergy triggers.

  • Improves indoor air quality
  • Enhances HVAC efficiency & reduces energy costs
  • Reduces dust and allergen buildup

Comprehensive HVAC Maintenance

Keep your HVAC system running at peak performance with our detailed inspection and tune-up services. We check filters, coils, motors, and other components to identify potential issues before they cause breakdowns, saving you costly repairs.

  • Increases system lifespan
  • Prevents unexpected breakdowns
  • Improves energy efficiency

Advanced Air Quality Testing

Our precise air quality assessments identify pollutants, mold spores, and allergens present in your home. We provide tailored recommendations based on test results to create a healthier living environment for your family.

  • Detects harmful airborne particles
  • Custom solutions to improve air quality
  • Peace of mind with professional testing

Mold & Contaminant Inspection

Mold can severely impact health and home integrity. Our expert inspection services identify hidden mold growth and other contaminants early, allowing for prompt remediation and safeguarding your home environment.

  • Early detection of mold & contaminants
  • Reduces risk of respiratory issues
  • Protects home structure and your health
