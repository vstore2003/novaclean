About NovaClean

Delivering superior indoor air quality and HVAC care across Halifax for over a decade.

Our Story

NovaClean was founded with a mission to improve the air we breathe. With over 10 years of experience in duct cleaning and HVAC maintenance, we’ve built a reputation for thorough, professional, and reliable service. Based in Halifax, our locally owned business is committed to helping homeowners and businesses enjoy cleaner, healthier environments.

Our Mission

To provide top-tier air duct and HVAC services using environmentally friendly methods and cutting-edge technology—delivering results that truly improve quality of life.

Our Core Values

  • Customer-first service and transparent communication
  • Eco-friendly, non-toxic cleaning practices
  • Certified expertise and ongoing technician training
  • Quality assurance and satisfaction guarantees
